About Death Note

Death Note, created by Tsugumi Ohba and Takeshi Obata, is a psychological thriller that explores themes of morality, justice, and power. The story follows Light Yagami, a brilliant high school student who discovers a mysterious notebook called the Death Note. The notebook grants him the ability to kill anyone simply by writing their name while picturing their face. Light decides to use it to rid the world of criminals and create a new world where he reigns as god. However, his actions attract the attention of L, a genius detective who is determined to stop him. What follows is an intense battle of wits, strategies, and deception. Death Note is celebrated for its gripping storyline, complex characters, and thought-provoking questions about what it means to play god.
