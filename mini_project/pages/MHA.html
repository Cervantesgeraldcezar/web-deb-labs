About My Hero Academia

My Hero Academia, written by Kohei Horikoshi, is a superhero-inspired manga and anime series set in a world where nearly everyone possesses a unique ability called a “Quirk.” The story follows Izuku Midoriya, a Quirkless boy who dreams of becoming a hero. His life changes when he inherits the legendary Quirk, One For All, from the world’s greatest hero, All Might. Enrolling in U.A. High School, Izuku trains alongside other aspiring heroes like Bakugo, Todoroki, and Uraraka, facing villainous organizations and dangerous challenges. The series is praised for blending Western superhero tropes with Japanese shonen storytelling, delivering themes of perseverance, courage, and friendship. My Hero Academia has become a modern classic in the anime world, inspiring films, video games, and a passionate global fanbase.
