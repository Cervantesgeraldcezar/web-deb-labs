About One Piece

One Piece (stylized in all caps) is a Japanese anime television series produced by Toei Animation that premiered on Fuji Television in October 1999. It is based on Eiichiro Oda's manga series of the same name. The story follows the adventures of Monkey D. Luffy, a 17-year-old young man whose body gained the properties of rubber after unintentionally eating a Devil Fruit. With his crew, named the Straw Hat Pirates, Luffy explores the Grand Line in search of the world's ultimate treasure known as the "One Piece" in order to become the next King of the Pirates.

Genre: Adventure, Fantasy comedy, Science fiction

Directed by: Kōnosuke Uda (#1–130; 196–263), Junji Shimizu (#131–195), Munehisa Sakai (#264–381), Hiroaki Miyamoto (#382–628), Toshinori Fukazawa (#629–891), Aya Komaki (#892–961), Kōhei Kureta (#892–1030), Tatsuya Nagamine (#892–1122), Satoshi Itō (#962–present), Yasunori Koyama (#1031–present), Wataru Matsumi (#1123–present)

Written by :Junki Takegami (#1–195), Hirohiko Kamisaka (#196–782), Shōji Yonemura (#783–present)

Original run: October 20, 1999 – present
