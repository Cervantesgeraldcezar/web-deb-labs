About Tokyo Ghoul

Tokyo Ghoul, written by Sui Ishida, is a dark fantasy manga series set in a world where flesh-eating ghouls live secretly among humans. The story centers on Ken Kaneki, an ordinary college student whose life changes after a fateful encounter with a ghoul. Following an accident, Kaneki undergoes surgery that unintentionally transforms him into a half-human, half-ghoul hybrid. Struggling with his new identity, he must navigate the dangerous world of ghouls while hiding his secret from humans. Tokyo Ghoul explores themes of identity, survival, morality, and the thin line between good and evil. Its mix of horror, psychological tension, and emotional depth has made it a cult classic among anime fans.
