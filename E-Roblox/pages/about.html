About the E-Roblox Learning Platform

Integrating game-based learning with formal education

The E-Roblox Learning Platform is an educational system designed to integrate Roblox game-based learning into classroom instruction. It aims to enhance student engagement, motivation, and understanding of academic concepts through interactive digital experiences.

Purpose of the Platform

This platform was developed to support teachers and students by providing an alternative learning approach that combines entertainment and education. It focuses on the subjects Mathematics, Science, and English, aligning learning activities with curriculum competencies and outcomes.

Why Roblox?

Roblox is a popular online platform that allows users to create and explore virtual environments. Its interactive nature promotes creativity, collaboration, problem-solving, and critical thinking, making it a suitable tool for educational instruction.

Educational Benefits

  • Encourages active and experiential learning
  • Improves student engagement and motivation
  • Supports collaboration and communication skills
  • Enhances understanding through visual and interactive tasks

Target Users

The E-Roblox Learning Platform is intended for:

  • Students in basic education
  • Teachers implementing game-based instruction
  • Researchers studying digital and game-based learning
